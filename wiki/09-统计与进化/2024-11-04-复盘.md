## Key Concepts

### 1. **断板日买入 (Buying on Broken Board Day)** — Not yet in wiki
- **Definition:** Buying a stock on the day it breaks its winning streak (涨停→not涨停). 思美传媒 was bought 10/31 when it broke board at 7.61.
- **Why it matters:** The document explicitly records this as a catastrophic error — the stock then dropped 26% over the weekend. This is a concrete, data-backed failure mode.
- **Wiki status:** Not present. Should be created as either a standalone concept or integrated into existing error pattern pages.

### 2. **亏损持仓过周末 (Holding Losers Over Weekend)** — Not yet in wiki
- **Definition:** Carrying a losing position into the weekend, exposing to gap risk with no ability to react.
- **Why it matters:** 思美传媒's -25.4% loss was exacerbated by holding through Saturday-Sunday. The 11/01→11/04 gap was devastating.
- **Wiki status:** Not present. Could be a sub-concept of risk management or error patterns.

### 3. **高位换手板追入 (Chasing High-Board Rotation Boards)** — Likely partially covered
- **Definition:** Buying a stock at 7连板 with margin, even when the board type has shifted from一字 to 换手 (indicating weakening).
- **Why it matters:** 华映科技 at 7连板换手 is the market's highest board. While it could continue, the risk-reward at 7 boards with margin is extremely unfavorable. Related to concepts of "高位博弈" already in wiki.
- **Wiki status:** Related to [[概念/高位博弈信号]] but more specific — this is about entering at extreme board heights with borrowed money.

### 4. **票执念/反复进出 (Ticker Obsession/Repeated Re-entry)** — Not yet in wiki
- **Definition:** The compulsion to repeatedly trade the same stock despite losing results. 银邦股份 3rd round trip: ±0, -6%, now re-entered.
- **Why it matters:** This is a distinct psychological pattern — not revenge trading exactly, but an inability to let go of a specific ticker. Wastes capital and mental bandwidth.
- **Wiki status:** Not present. Distinct from [[概念/锚定效应]] (which is about price anchoring). This is about identity/relationship with a specific stock.

### 5. **龙头信仰与板块内部分化 (Leader Faith vs. Intra-Sector Divergence)** — Emergent concept
- **Definition:** The market can simultaneously show "faith" in some leaders (常山北明涨停反包, 欧菲光涨停) while other former leaders (海能达, 宗申动力, 电广传媒) continue collapsing. Not a uniform recovery.
- **Why it matters:** The user correctly identifies this as "板块内部剧烈分化." This is a more nuanced market reading than simple "华为链复活" — it's selective revival.
- **Wiki status:** Not present. Could connect to [[概念/内部缩圈]] but with a different angle (缩圈 implies narrowing; this is about internal schism within a theme).

### 6. **最高板断板信号 (Highest Board Break Signal)** — Not yet in wiki
- **Definition:** When the market's absolute highest board (松发股份 12连板) breaks, it signals cycle turning. 924后最高连板纪录终结 is a macro cycle event.
- **Why it matters:** The user notes this carefully. It contextualizes why buying 华映科技 at 7连板 is risky — the cycle's ceiling just got defined at 12.
- **Wiki status:** Not present. Could be a sub-section of [[概念/退潮日]] or a standalone concept about cycle measurement.
