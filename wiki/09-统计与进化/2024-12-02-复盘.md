## Key Concepts

### 1. 断板低吸 (Broken-Board Pullback Buying)
- **Definition**: Buying after a stock breaks its limit-up streak (断板) and pulls back, anticipating a rebound.
- **Why it matters**: User explicitly labels the 粤桂 trade as "断板低吸逻辑完整执行" — a strategy executed start to finish (11/22 buy → 11/25 sell → 11/28 rebuy → 12/02 sell). This is a **positive pattern** in user's history.
- **Wiki status**: ❌ No dedicated page. The concept appears in trade descriptions but lacks formalization as a strategy with entry/exit rules.

### 2. 极致偏多环境 (Extreme Bullish Environment)
- **Definition**: 182 limit-ups (2nd highest post-9/24), only 5 limit-downs, all five major indices up.
- **Why it matters**: User correctly identifies this as a "全面爆发期" (full breakout phase), not just normal bullishness. This is the kind of environment where the user historically over-trades (per 12月亏损模式诊断).
- **Wiki status**: ❌ Not in wiki; could be a sub-concept under market sentiment or added to 市场过热信号.

### 3. 一线龙头封单量 (Leader Lock-up Volume as Signal)
- **Definition**: Using the absolute size of opening lock-up orders (封单额) to gauge genuine market conviction vs. hype.
- **Evidence**: User tracks it systematically — 山东矿机 12亿, 利欧 18亿, 广博 3.1亿. These numbers measure real capital commitment.
- **Why it matters**: This is a **sophisticated market reading skill** that the user demonstrates but doesn't formalize.
- **Wiki status**: ❌ Not in wiki.

### 4. 断板A杀 (Post-Break A-Kill)
- **Definition**: After a stock's limit-up streak breaks, it immediately gaps down and continues falling ("A" shape on chart).
- **Evidence**: 锦龙 4连板→跌停, 瑞斯康达 4连板→跌停, 魅视第2天跌停.
- **Why it matters**: User notes this but dismisses it as "涨停潮压倒负反馈" — a **potentially dangerous dismissal**. A-kill signals are early warnings even in bull environments.
- **Wiki status**: ❌ Not explicitly in wiki, though related to 退潮日 concepts.

### 5. The "换仓" (Position Switch) Pattern
- **Observation**: User exits a winning trade (粤桂 +3.1%, +2,014元) and immediately enters a new chase-style trade (川发, 高位追涨停). This is the **exact same structure** as prior problematic patterns.
- **Wiki connection**: Directly links to [[概念/割肉-追涨循环]], though here it's "止盈-追涨" variant.

### Caveat User Missed
The user notes "断板A杀明显，但整体涨停潮压倒负反馈" (A-kill is obvious but overwhelmed by bull). This is **true for 12/02** but dangerous as a heuristic — when the tide turns, those A-kill signals that were "overwhelmed" become the new reality. The user should log this as: "A-kill signals exist → environment is masking them → if environment shifts, these stocks will lead the decline."
